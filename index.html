ABOUT CHRIST LOVE
INTERNATIONAL CHURCH

OUR VISION IS TO CONNECT ANYONE ANYWHERE TO A LIFE‑CHANGING RELATIONSHIP WITH JESUS

We are wholeheartedly committed to building a church that reaches people who don't yet know God.
We present the timeless message of the gospel in modern and relevant ways.The message is sacred and not to be altered,
but our approach and method is always open to change. We are a Bible-believing church with a theology and beliefs that are the same as hundreds of thousands of churches around the world.
